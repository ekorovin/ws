Overview

Lower costs, increase reliability, and focus on your business instead of ours.

Simeio Business-Ready IAM Cloud provides a simple and low-cost alternative to building and maintaining your own IAM infrastructure.Our Identity-as-a-Service (IaaS) solution eliminates the hardware costs, in many cases the software licenses, plus the ongoing maintenance and upgrade costs required to deploy and maintain an IAM infrastructure.

You get lower costs, faster time to market, enhanced security, greater flexibility and, perhaps most important, the freedom to focus on the value and quality of the services you provide instead of how they're provided.

End-to-End Identity Services

End-to-End Identity Services

Simeio Business-Ready IAM Cloud offers complete, end-to-end identity services tailored to your specific requirements, including:

  • Identity administration
  • Access request
  • Role and access governance
  • Identity risk and intelligence

It provides automated workflows for user access request submission and review, provisioning and attestation. It enables federation. And it simplifies compliance with regulatory mandates.

Simeio Business-Ready IAM Cloud uses best-of-breed IAM products including Oracle, CA and Forge Rock. It runs on Amazon's EC2 cloud infrastructure for the ultimate in performance, security, scalability and reliability. It's available in both single and multi-tenant deployments.

Fast and EasyDeployment

Fast and Easy Deployment

Simeio Business-Ready IAM Cloud can be deployed in a fraction of the time of a traditional IAM implementation.

By installing our proprietary Identity Interceptor appliances at your location, we can quickly and seamlessly integrate your existing enterprise applications and identity stores. These pre-configured appliances enable highly secure, bi-directional communications between your IT infrastructure and the Simeio Business-Ready IAM Cloud using AES/3DES encryption and customer-specific keys. Because it supports widely adopted industry standards such as SPML, SAML and OAuth, you can even integrate other SaaS applications.
