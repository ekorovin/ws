Simeio Solutions Achieves Oracle PartnerNetwork Specialization in Identity Administration and Analytics

Simeio Joins Only a Handful of Global Companies to Have Achieved This Milestone

HOBOKEN, NJ--(Marketwire - Jul 12, 2011) - Simeio Solutions (www.simeiosolutions.com), a leading consulting, services and solutions provider, today announced it has achieved Oracle Identity Administration and Analytics Specialization. Simeio Solutions is among the first few companies globally to receive the specialization, joining a small and select group of Oracle Partners. To achieve this milestone, Simeio demonstrated the required knowledge, competency, and implementation expertise in the Oracle Fusion Middleware stack, specifically, in the areas of identity provisioning, role management, access governance and compliance.

"This specialization differentiates Simeio in the marketplace and provides us with a competitive edge," said Hemen Vimadalal, CEO and managing partner, Simeio Solutions. "The fact that we are one of only a handful of companies in the world with Oracle's Identity Administration and Analytics Specialization truly validates the results we've been able to produce for our clients, many of whom represent the Fortune 50. Customer references played a significant part in achieving specialization, and that in itself is one of the greatest honors we could hope to achieve."

Simeio Solutions is an Oracle Gold Partner that serves global customers in North America, Canada, Australia, Asia, Europe and the UK. Simeio also received an Honorable Mention in the Security and Identity Management Solution category for the Oracle North America Titan Awards 2010, which recognized Oracle's business partners for innovative and successful uses of Oracle technology. This further demonstrates Simeio's excellence in solving real-world customer challenges and for their development and deployment of Oracle technology.

About Simeio Solutions
Simeio Solutions, LLC is a global systems integration and compliance services company specializing in enterprise security consulting, business process, implementation, managed services and Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) in the areas of identity and access management (IAM), role-based access controls (RBAC), and IT governance, risk and compliance (IT-GRC). Simeio is headquartered in Hoboken, New Jersey. For more information, visit www.simeiosolutions.com.
