Gartner Positions Simeio Solutions As A Niche Player In Magic Quadrant Report For IDaaS

“Niche vendors may be best for your needs.”June 6, 2014

Hoboken, NJ – June 06, 2014 – Simeio Solutions has been placed in the Niche Players Quadrant by Gartner, Inc. in the just released Magic Quadrant for Identity and Access Management as a Service (IDaaS) Report*.

According to Gartner, “Vendors come from distinctly different backgrounds, and there are significant variances among providers with regard to IAM functional depth and support provided for different use cases. Niche vendors may be best for your needs.”

Simeio CEO Hemen Vimadalal explains, “The IDaaS market is still evolving, and our customers face rapidly growing IAM challenges that require specialized skills to meet today’s need for enhanced security, greater flexibility and faster time to market. Our Business-Ready IAM Cloud uses best-of-breed IAM technologies combined with our proprietary Simeio Identity Intelligence Center™ (IIC) to provide one of the most powerful and flexible IDaaS offerings.”

According to Vimadalal, “We believe our position in the Magic Quadrant reflects our strength in providing a range of products that provide deep functional support for Web and legacy applications combined with the actionable business insights that Simeio IIC provides.”
Simeio Solutions provides Web access management (WAM), identity administration, access request, role and compliance, risk intelligence, directory services, and IT governance, risk and compliance (GRC).

*Gartner “Magic Quadrant for Identity and Access Management as a Service (IDaaS)” by Gregg Kreizman. June 2, 2014.

About Simeio Solutions

Simeio Solutions, Inc. offers complete, end-to-end Identity and Access Management (IAM) services and solutions powered by the Simeio Identity Intelligence Center™ (IIC). A single-source for all your identity and access management needs, our offering spans plan to build to operate, with a la carte access to professional services (strategy and implementation), business-ready IAM cloud solutions, and expert managed services for on-going operational support.
