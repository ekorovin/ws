Overview

Lower your costs, increase your agility and flexibility, and improve your quality of service

In today's interconnected world, you must be able to quickly and easily provide employees, partners and customers with secure and role-appropriate access to a rapidly evolving set of information, applications and other online resources, using a wide variety of devices from anywhere in the world.

This requires monitoring and remediation at unprecedented levels of service. It requires multiple subject matter experts (SMEs) with rare and highly specialized skills. The challenge can overwhelm even the most adroit IT organization.

You provide the infrastructure, we handle the rest.

Our Expert Managed Services, powered by Simeio IIC, can transform the way you deliver security and identity. Whether on-premise or remotely managed, we can handle the complete, end-to-end management of your Identity and Access Management (IAM) infrastructure, including:

  • Reliable 24/7 support
  • Day-to-day operations such as infrastructure sizing, development, integration and implementation
  • Solution architecture and design
  • Technology evaluation and recommendation
  • Project management

On Premise

On Premise

Our seasoned team of identity professionals can provide complete on-site operations and infrastructure support for your IAM systems. We work side-by-side with your IT team to help ensure day-to-day operations of these specialized systems are painless and trouble free.

We can handle:

  • provisioning and de-provisioning
  • manage trouble tickets
  • oversee software installations or migrations
  • and more

And we can help evaluate, design and build IAM solutions to meet your evolving needs.

Remotely Managed

Remotely Managed

We offer a powerful and flexible approach to managed services by providing operations and infrastructure support offsite on an on-demand basis. Investment in resources at your location is completely eliminated. You get all the benefits of a dedicated team of IAM professionals without the overhead.

Powered by Simeio IIC™

Powered by Simeio IIC™

Whether on premise or remotely managed, our expert managed services leverage the power of Simeio IIC to raise the level of service you can expect to new heights.

No matter how complex your IAM infrastructure is, integration with Simeio IIC couldn’t be easier. We install our proprietary Identity Interceptor appliances at your location. These pre-configured appliances enable highly secure, bi-directional communications between your IT infrastructure and Simeio IIC using AES/3DES encryption and customer-specific keys. This allows us to manage and control all of your IAM systems from a single interface, giving us unprecedented insights into performance, security, and more.

The Simeio IIC advantage
Increased reliability, lower cost and greater insights

Simeio IIC can dramatically simplify operations by providing a single consistent view of your IAM systems regardless of the complexity of the underlying technologies. This enables us to deliver more reliable managed services at a lower cost – especially in today’s heterogeneous IAM environments.

You also get increased insights into how accounts are being used – or misused – and analytics that can help identify new business opportunities
