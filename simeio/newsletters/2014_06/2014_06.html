Round, Round Get Around, We Get Around


Simeio recently presented at two CISO Executive Network roundtable discussions in Toronto and New York City. Simeio’s Asif Savvas presented in Toronto, while Anshuman Chakravatty presented at the New York City event, both of which were filled with leading information security, IT risk management, privacy, and compliance executives. The topic: how can companies know what they are getting from their investment in information security, specifically focusing on measuring, analyzing and reporting. While the starting premise was that it was unrealistic to expect security ROI, we were happy to report that the Simeio Identity Intelligence Center™ (IIC™) was designed not only to help companies manage and defend their IAM infrastructures, but also to provide actionable business intelligence.
