Overview

We are with you every step of the way

From strategy to implementation, Simeio Solutions delivers successful Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions utilizing methodologies that have been proven in a wide array of client environments, including Fortune 100 enterprises.

Our approach is highly flexible and tailored to meet the specific needs of each customer. From initial needs assessment and strategy development through solution design and implementation, we are focused on ensuring information and system security, user friendliness and cost efficiency.

An Enhanced Approach

An enhanced approach to IAM planning

We work with each customer to develop a future-state IAM strategy and tactical steps to achieve that strategy. Our teams of experts are skilled at mapping Simeio Solutions best practices methodologies for key considerations, such as:

  • User lifecycle management
  • Role lifecycle management
  • User access governance
  • Risk management and segregation of duties
  • Directory services
  • Authentication management
  • Fine-grained authorization and entitlement management
 

Solutions Implementation

Solutions ImplementationPowered by Simeio IICTM
