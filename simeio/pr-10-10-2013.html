Global Association of Investment Professionals to Use CA Technologies Identity and Access Governance Solution Delivered with Simeio Cloud Service

HOBOKEN, N.J. and ISLANDIA, NY — September 9, 2013 — Simeio Solutions and CA Technologies (NASDAQ: CA) today announced that CFA Institute, the global association of investment professionals, has selected CA SiteMinder®, CA IdentityMinder™, and CA GovernanceMinder™, delivered through Simeio Cloud Services, to help securely improve operations and productivity while providing improved services and a positive user experience to employees, partners and more than 500,000 candidates, members and other stakeholders in over 100 countries globally.

To achieve the improvements and deliver a simple, automated user experience, CFA Institute turned to CA Technologies and Simeio Solutions for a seamless, scalable identity and access management (IAM) system that provides the foundation for a trusted IT environment using:

  • Identity administration, automated user provisioning and de-provisioning and user self-service;
  • Identity and access governance managing entitlements, certification, segregation of duties and role management; and
  • Federation / single sign-on across internal and external CFA Institute applications.

“The IAM service from Simeio, backed by CA Technologies IAM solutions, supports both our long-term business and technology strategy to provide services and infrastructure for optimum online engagement and transaction with CFA Institute,” said Elaine Cheng, CFA Institute chief information officer. “Identity and access governance is critical for our organization’s members and candidates who need access to CFA Institute resources, but also value security of personal information. Managing their identities and governing their access is critical to upholding the very basis of our mission – ethics and professional excellence.”

Shawn Keve, partner, Simeio Solutions, said, “Cloud-based IAM from Simeio will provide CFA Institute with efficiencies through reduced infrastructure spend, reduced implementation time and improved productivity. In addition, the scalability and streamlined deployment of the Simeio Cloud Service allows for the anticipated growth of CFA Institute members and desire to add new services.”

Simeio Cloud Services are delivered in partnership with CA Technologies. “Simeio Solutions special focus on delivering the capabilities of CA Technologies IAM solutions in the cloud, reflects our long-standing commitment to our serving our customers, while delivering best of breed technology,” said Ed Pascua, SVP at Simeio Solutions.

“Simeio is a Premier partner for CA Technologies with a strong consulting and technology acumen in the IAM marketplace,” said CA David Bradley, senior vice president, Global Partner Sales, CA Technologies. “We have worked successfully together to deploy our identity and access governance solutions, including CA SiteMinder, CA IdentityMinder and CA GovernanceMinder. We are excited about this latest opportunity with CFA Institute which will showcase the combined strength of Simeio’s cloud-based service with our leading IAM technology.”
