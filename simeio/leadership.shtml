Hemen VimadalalManaging Partner / CEO

Hemen Vimadalal is the Managing Partner / CEO at Simeio Solutions and the identity and access management (IAM) practice lead for Simeio Solutions. He drives the vision and overall business strategy for Simeio Solutions.

He was with VAAU from its inception where he helped establish the company to be positioned as the leader in the identity management industry. He has led many assessments and end-to-end implementations of role-based access control, user provisioning, identity auditing, identity certification, access control, web services, directory and password management products.

Recognized as a thought leader in the identity management space, he regularly speaks at professional conferences and has published several whitepapers around IAM. In addition, he is knowledgeable about the regulatory and compliance requirements of Sarbanes-Oxley, HIPAA, GLBA, and similar statutes as they relate to user provisioning, administration, access control and segregation of duties.

Naynesh PatelPartner

Naynesh Patel is a Partner with Simeio Solutions. In his position, he will shape and guide Simeio’s business strategies and operations, as well as lead the company’s technology and product direction.

He joins Simeio from KPMG LLP, where he served as practice director and Americas leader for identity and access management (IAM) services. During his tenure with KPMG, he led the development of, and served in a national instructor capacity, for the global delivery methodology around identity and access management.

Naynesh has over 15 years of experience in the implementation, strategy, design and assessment of identity and access management solutions for clients in the telecom, retail, energy, automotive, pharmaceutical, food and beverage, entertainment, financial services and insurance sectors. He is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and holds a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Kennesaw State University.

Shawn KevePartner

Shawn Keve is a Partner with Simeio Solutions. He plays a key role in helping to grow Simeio’s business and to expand Simeio’s mindshare in the marketplace.

Most recently, he was Consulting Director at Oracle and Director of Professional Services for Sun Microsystems prior to Oracle’s acquisition of Sun. He was responsible for the Sales and Delivery of a nearly $100M portfolio of IT services. Before joining Sun, he was part of Netscape eCommerce Solutions (acquired by AOL), KPMG Consulting, and MIT Lincoln Laboratory. Shawn has over 18 years of experience in the strategy, architecture, design, and implementation of enterprise solutions across several industries, including financial services, health care and life sciences, manufacturing, media and entertainment, telecommunications, and retail. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration (Management Information Systems) from Northeastern University.

Asif SavvasAssociate Partner

Asif Savvas is an Associate Partner with Simeio Solutions. He is responsible for sales and delivery for the Simeio Canada IAM practice. Prior to joining Simeio, Asif held responsibility for delivery of identity solutions across Canada at Oracle/Sun.

At Oracle, he contributed to growing the identity footprint in Canada. Asif played a unique role of a solutions advisor to some of Oracle’s largest customers in Canada. He joined Sun as part of the Vaau acquisition where he played a vital role in establishing the Vaau Role Management Product (now renamed Oracle Identity Analytics) as an industry leader in the areas of Role Management and Compliance. He has worked across North America serving clients in the Utility, energy, telecommunications, and financial services industries and is seen as thought leader in the areas of Identity Management, RBAC and Compliance.

He holds a B.Tech in Information Technology from Madras University and an MBA from The University of British Columbia’s, Sauder School of Business.
