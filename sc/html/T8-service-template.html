      Associated Programs

      Teen & Young Adult Clinic

      The Teen and Young Adult Clinic provides comprehensive, confidential health services, including reproductive health counseling and sexually transmitted infection testing.

      Mondays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays
      1:00pm - 5:00 pm


      Pediatric Weight Clinic

      Packard Children's Hospital's Pediatric Weight Clinic helps overweight and obese children and teens improve their health and quality of life.

      Fridays
      8:00 am - 12:00 pm
      Phone: (650) 498-4468

      Comprehensive Eating Disorders Program

      The Comprehensive Eating Disorders Program provides medical, nutritional and psychiatric treatment for adolescents with anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa and eating disorders not otherwise specified (EDNOS).

      Mondays, Tuesdays and Fridays
      12:00pm - 6:00 pm


      Adolescent Health Van

      The Adolescent Health Van provides health services for uninsured and/or homeless youth by visiting various San Francisco, San Mateo and Santa Clara county locations.

      Tuesdays and Thursdays
