Simeio Solutions Cited as a Strong Performer by Independent Research Firm

“The Forrester Wave™: Enterprise Cloud Identity and Access Management, Q3 2012: Six Vendors That Matter Most and How They Stack Up.”

Hoboken, NJ – July 23, 2012 – Simeio Solutions (www.simeiosolutions.com), provider of a comprehensive suite of enterprise Identity and Access Management (IAM) resources designed to offer a fully integrated solution, was among the select companies that Forrester Research, Inc., invited to participate in its July 19, 2012, report, “The Forrester Wave™: Enterprise Cloud Identity and Access Management, Q3 2012: Six Vendors That Matter Most and How They Stack Up.” In this evaluation, Simeio Solutions was cited as a Strong Performer.

The Forrester Wave report included key factors driving the need for cloud-based IAM, the degree of specialization reflected in the current market, and multi-point evaluations of solution providers. Market drivers noted in the report include trends toward extended enterprises and rapid proliferation of cloud apps. Solution drivers include targeting of a certain population, protected resources and IAM feature sets.

Solution providers were evaluated on the basis of 15 criteria grouped into three high-level categories: current offering, strategy and market presence. Simeio Solutions was rated as a Strong Performer overall with specific strengths in user account provisioning into applications, access recertification and delegated administration, user repositories, and auditing and reporting.

In addition, according to the report, Simeio Solutions “offers many wizards for simple policy definition, implicit and just-in-time provisioning, and strong analytics and reporting capabilities, as well as outstanding attestation based on Oracle Identity Analytics.”

“We are very proud to have been selected for inclusion in this prestigious Forrester Wave report and cited as a Strong Performer,” said Simeio Solutions Managing Partner Hemen Vimadalal. “Forrester Research is one of the preeminent research and advisory firms in the world, and we know that the marketplace relies on them for independent insight to make important strategic and product decisions.”

Simeio Solutions is a unique strategic partner offering a comprehensive suite of enterprise Identity and Access Management resources designed to provide a fully integrated solution that meets the specific needs of each client. The company’s single-source approach to IAM encompasses managed services (both on-premise and remotely managed), cloud-based identity-as-a-service, and professional services that ensure on-target, smooth and cost-effective system design and implementation.
